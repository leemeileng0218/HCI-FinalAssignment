Min Nature Kuala Lumpur (Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia)

Min Nature Kuala Lumpur

Located in the heart of Malaysia’s capital, Miniature Kuala Lumpur is a 1:25 scale park showcasing over 50 iconic landmarks, including the Petronas Twin Towers, KL Tower, Merdeka Square, and Sultan Abdul Samad Building. Each model features intricate details, miniature vehicles, people, and working lights, with interactive panels sharing the history and significance of each site.

Highlights:

  • 360° panoramic city view
  • Children’s play area and mini construction toys
  • 4D cinema showing KL’s transformation
  • Gift shop and café with Malaysian snacks

    • Getting There:

    Walking distance from Merdeka Square or Masjid Jamek Station; taxis and ride-hailing also available. Fully wheelchair- and stroller-accessible. Guided tours in multiple languages.
