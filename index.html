I’m Kate Lee, a traveller from Malaysia. This blog is where I document the destination

I’ve visited and share practical information experiences, and guides to help you plan

your own journeys—whether around Malaysia or abroad. Malaysia is a beautiful and diverse country

in Southeast Asia, known for its rich culture, friendly people, and delicious food. It is

made up of Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia on the island of Borneo. Malaysia offers a mix

of modern cities, tropical beaches, rainforests, and historical sites. Popular places include

Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Malacca, Sabah, and Sarawak.
