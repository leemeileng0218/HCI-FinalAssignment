I’m Kate Lee, a traveller from Malaysia.

This blog is where I document the destination

I’ve visited and share practical information

experiences, and guides to help you plan

your own journeys—whether around Malaysia or abroad.

Malaysia is a beautiful and diverse country

in Southeast Asia, known for its rich culture,

friendly people, and delicious food. It is

made up of Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia

on the island of Borneo. Malaysia offers a mix

of modern cities, tropical beaches, rainforests,

and historical sites. Popular places include

Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Malacca, Sabah, and Sarawak.

Malaysia has a convenient and affordable transportation

system. In major cities like Kuala Lumpur,

you can travel easily using trains such as

the MRT, LRT, and monorail. Buses and taxis

are widely available, and e-hailing services

like Grab are commonly used. For longer distances,

you can travel by intercity buses, domestic flights,

or trains such as KTM. Highways also

make road trips simple and accessible.
