Cui Lake Kunming (China)

Green Lake (Cuihu Park), Kunming

Green Lake (Cuihu Park) is a peaceful oasis in the heart of Kunming, surrounded by weeping willows, landscaped gardens, and historic pavilions such as the Daguan Pavilion from the Qing Dynasty. Stone bridges connect small islands across the lake, while teahouses and snack kiosks line the shore, where locals gather to practice tai chi, fly kites, or enjoy traditional music.

Nearby attractions include Yunnan University, known for its colonial-era buildings, and the Cuihu Park Zoo, home to Yunnan’s famous golden monkeys. Boat rides—ranging from paddle boats to traditional wooden boats—offer a relaxing way to explore the lake.

Kunming’s mild “Spring City” climate makes Green Lake enjoyable year-round, with cool winters and warm, pleasant summers. The park is centrally located, easy to reach by metro or bus, and fully walkable with paved paths around the lake. Visit early morning for a calm atmosphere or late afternoon for beautiful sunsets, and don’t miss local snacks like rice noodles and flower cakes nearby.
