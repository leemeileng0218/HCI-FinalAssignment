"Here is the checklist to prepare before your trip."

  1. Book flights and hotels early using trusted platforms like Booking.com, Expedia, or Trip.com.
  2. Plan your first night accommodation carefully.
  3. Photocopy important documents and store them online.
  4. Prepare adapters, SIM cards, and local currency.
  5. Notify your bank and credit card provider.
  6. Check international roaming with your carrier.
  7. Install a translation app for convenience.
