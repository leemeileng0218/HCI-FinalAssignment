"Here is the checklist to prepare before your trip."
  1. For a smooth and budget-friendly travel experience, consider booking your flights and hotels through trusted platforms like Booking.com, Expedia.com, or Trip.com — they offer great deals, user reviews, and flexible options to suit your itinerary.

  2. Plan your first night down to the last detail.
  3. Photocopy your driver's license, passport, visa, and important documents. Save them in the cloud.
  4. Buy adapters, SIM cards, and currency before departure.
  5. Alert your credit card and bank about your travel.
  6. Check with your phone carrier for international service.
  7. Consider using a translation app.
