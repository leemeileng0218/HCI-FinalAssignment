3rd Floor Tea House Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia)

I had a pleasant and memorable experience at the 3rd Floor Tea House in Kuala Lumpur.

The moment I stepped in, the atmosphere felt calm and welcoming, with soft music and a

cozy interior that made it easy to relax. The staff were friendly and attentive,

adding to the warm environment. I enjoyed a variety of tea and light snacks,

each served beautifully. The fragrance of the tea and the peaceful setting made

the experience feel almost like a short escape from the busy city below.

Sitting by the window and looking out over the street added to the charm.

On the other side, there was a cozy craft section filled with handmade crochet

bags, flowers, and cute knitted toys. The colorful yarns, neatly arranged shelves,

and charming handmade pieces created a warm and welcoming vibe. It felt like

stepping into a small creative community where people freely express themselves

through art and crafts. Walking around this area made the whole experience at

the tea house even more unique. It wasn’t just a place to relax with tea—it felt

like a hidden spot where art, creativity, and comfort come together.
